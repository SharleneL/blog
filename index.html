Learn to Use VSCO Film Presets in Lightroom - VSCO Film 07

Eclectic Films features a wide range of looks in our largest release to date, with 18 films and over 100 presets in total. The spectrum of this collection translates to versatility in use and includes muted, vivid, tungsten-balanced, and black and white films — all of which embody an elegant and modern aesthetic. This pack is ideal for most settings and subjects, particularly portraits, night photography, and architecture.

VSCO's official description of Film 07



General Features

VSCO Film 07 is officially described as “Eclectic Films”. Based on personal understanding, this set of presets has the following main features:

  • Subtle effects for general purpose: that’s why VSCO describes it as “versatile”. 07 contains a wide range of film-looks. The effects of 07 is not too strong (as 03 or 04), and can be applied on multiple photography types (but not specifically for portrait, landscape, etc). However, 07 also contains several uniquely strong looks.
  • Less grains, low fade: films in this set all have relatively low ISO (25~320). Thus there’s not lots of grains or fade.

Each film in 07 contains 7 versions: normal, -, –, +, ++, Cool, Warm.

Film Features

1. Agfa Optima 100 II

Film Type: Professional color negative film
Saturation: Fairly high; but still preserving natural colors
Color Balance: Warm, deep red tones; adds warm-saturated tone to daylight images
Overview: Adds extra lively effects without dramatic changes; would intensify shadows but still maintain great color depth with life-like colors.

Best for: General purpose.

2. Agfa Portrait XPS 160

Film Type: Professional negative film
Contrast: Medium
Saturation: Muted
Color Balance: A bit warm; adds complementary colors (pastel green & blue) for skin tones. Brighter yellow.

Best for: High range portrait situations (such as outdoor portraits); Weddings. Also good for bright portraits.
Not suitable for: low range scenes (low contrast will make image flat and dull).

3. Agfa RSX 50 II

Film Type: Professional slide film
Contrast: Mild
Color Balance: Neutral colors

4. Agfa RSX 200 II

Film Type: Professional slide film
Contrast: Low
Saturation: Fairly low; muted
Color Balance: A warm, yellow tint; muted green
Overview: RSX50 & RSX200 have subtle enhancement on the image; it brings out the details in shadows and highlights; having a retro and archival quality.

Best for: General purpose. Portraits would be detailed & textures; landscapes would be dry & arid.

[TRY IT] for low key green & retro & documentary & detailed portrait; for arid landscape; especially good for light green nature, will produce yellow green; try with mist days; warm++ version is very good for forest & nature wedding portraits. Better for images with rich texture, or the image would be plain and dull.

5. Agfa Ultra 50 & 100

Film Type: Professional negative film
Contrast: Noticable contrast bump
Saturation: Highly saturated color
Color Balance: Strong blues & reds; Ultra 50 is also strong on yellows
Overview: Both have an opposite effect of RSX 200 and are great for a bolder look with brighter colors, also good for skin tones.

Best for: General purpose. Compare with Agfa Optima: choose Agfa Ultra if you need more saturated blues & greens.

[TRY IT] for deeper green & yellow highlight & forest & nature; for vivid & saturated feeling

Saturation: Ultra 50&100 > Optima 100 > Portrait 160

6. Fuji 160S

Film Type: Professional negative film
Saturation: Low
Contrast: Low
Color Balance: More mild and cool color palette than Agfa Portrait XPS160; noticable deep reds; muted blue; adds green tint for greater skin tone
Overview: A portrait focused film. A lower contrast & lower saturated version of Fuji 160C in Film 01 (“S” means sharp, “C” means contrast). 160S has more natural skin tone & gray balance, as well as less vibrancy; clean and cool. ++ version might be more similar to real 160S film.

Best for: More understated portrait look (which is similar to RSX200).

[TRY IT] for low key portraits; try its cool version.

7. Fuji Sensia 100

Film Type: Consumer slide film
Color Balance: Strong reds & slightly warm toned; yellow & green tint; muted blue; green is deep & lush which really stands out beside the generally muted colors.
Overview: Deep blacks, more unnatural and unpredictable colors comparing with the professional slide films; + and ++ version seem like expired film; the Alt version has some interesting color shifts.

Best for: Evokes a dismal & grim feeling; propably suitable for urban cityscape photography. (荒芜的乡村)

[TRY IT] for sad, dismal feeling; also walk for portraits

8. Illford Pan F Plus 50

Very clean BW film; has a “tremendous depth”; a slow speed film thus the grains are fine and sutble yet still add texture to the image.

9. Kodak Ektachrome 64

Film Type: Professional Slide Film
Contrast: Medium
Saturation: Medium
Color Balance: Natural; warm with more red tones for pleasant skin tones; evident intensified blue
Overview: A clean, distinctive looked film; old and famous slide film also known as “EPR”; used to be very popular with fashion and documentary photography. Rich, natural colors and soft highlight contrast. It’s color palette is cooler and contrast less intense than it’s famous sibling, Kodachrome. Has an Alt version with higher saturation.

Best for: General purpose for a clean, distinctive look, though extremely popular with fashion, landscape and documentary. Documentary portraits (with red tone & more texture), but not for a “glowing” skin tone which is saught in wedding portraits.

[TRY IT] warm version for retro portraits & nature & landscape; Alt version is also good for wedding portraits; warm & brighter yellow

10. Kodak Ektar 25

Film Type: Professional negative film
Color Balance: A bit warm color but with softer blues than the Ektachrome
Overview: ISO25 thus the grain is extremely low; slow speed makes it might have a glowing skin tone. Ektar was known for having incredible colors & clarity, as well as virtually no film grain. Deeper darkness, brighter colors, glowing skin tones. Deeper the darkness & add brown to skin tone.

Best for: VERY general purpose. Can hardly get wrong cause it’s subtleness.

11. Kodak Elite 50

Film Type: Consumer slide film
Contrast: Medium; deeper blacks

12. Kodak Plus-X 125

Very even BW film; nice open shadows & soft highlights; less contrast than Illford Pan F & a bit more grain.

13. Kodak TRI-X 320

Similar to the much-loved TRI-X400; but more popular in studios with under more controlled lighting; high contrast; this film is only available for medium format, thus the grain is finer than 35mm film. Between Illford Pan F & Plus-X

Contrast: Illford Pan F > TRI-X 320 > Plus-X 125

14. Night-Tungsten Films

Unlike in other presets packs, these films are not night balanced versions of daylight films, but are films which are designed to be shot at night; very blue; help to cut down on the luminance of reds and yellows for a more natural looking; low speed, originally for architectural and industrial photography. The white balance is based on tungsten light source. To use these presets, need to manually adjust white-balance to 3200K to get the correct white balance. Including:

  • Fuji T64: most dramatic one; fairly stron gyellow or greenish tint; high contrast; the + version deviate from the color tint quite a bit.
  • Kodak Ektachrome 64T: opposite effect with T64; low contrast; cool tone. Very subtle, low contrast Tungsten film with neutral colors. This is a good choice for room interiors & artwork.
  • Kodak Elite Chrome 160T: interesting effect with medium contrast; a balance between cool shadows & warm highlights; the Alt versions provide more unique & bolder looks. Good for stark & dismal indoor (like raw walls under construction).
  • Kodak Portra 100T: the only negative tungsten film in this group; warm color; fairly understated color palette. Very muted pastel colors with a strong greenish-blue tint.

consumer film vs professional film:
Reference
One of the biggest difference with professional film is that more care was put into ensuring neutral color balance. What does that mean? Well, analog film’s color balance changes over time, so manufactures would “age” the professional film and ship it right when it’s color balance was ideal. Retailers would then immediately refrigerate the professional film to keep it in balance.

For the purpose of VSCO Film 07, this means that these professional films have a higher chance of having the originally intended neutral color balance, assuming VSCO could get their hands on film stock that had been properly preserved. Even with these precautions, it’s likely the film’s colors have shifted a bit. But it’s way closer than they are likely to get with consumer films.

Beyond color balance and handling, Professional films typically favor more natural color saturation and contrast. But this varies by film.

Consumer films are more likely to be expired and off balance from a color perspective. Why? Simply because manufacturers and retailers didn’t put the same care into them. Retailers shipped them early (before they were ideally balanced) with the assumption that it would take a few months before they were sold and used by consumer enthusiasts.

There was also a tendency in consumer films to add a bit more contrast, which added “pop” but meant loss of detail in shadows and highlights.

关于住宿

舍Airbnb其谁😊。本人大义祭出实习时出卖血汗换来的\$500 travel credit，以及不知何时获得的\$50 referral credit——为了组织的幸福，我高风亮节！而且我司教导我们与朋友share credit，我觉得如果我司的东西能让我的朋友们开心那我也会非常开心的:D。所以我们四个人住宿一共消耗\$130。

吾四人首次同游，心中澎湃有加，so must find a 靠谱房子。分享一下我的找房步骤：

  1. 马景点：从穷游/知乎/Airbnb Guidebook的各种列表中找到所有可能去的地方，mark在Google maps上。不管最后是不是真的会去，先马起来。马多了就能大致明白城市的哪几个区域比较值得逛。
  2. 找房子：这样就大概确定旅行范围有多大，可以开始在Airbnb上找交通便利的房源了。
  3. 确定安全状况：记得确定房源所在区域是否安全。Google搜一下所去城市的crime map，可以看到城市里各个区域的近期犯罪情况。

芝加哥downtown的房子都有点太贵了（毕竟我仅持$550负责四口人三晚住宿），根据我们的研究，北部Lake View区是交通方便房价公道且治安良好的上佳选择之一。无奈前一天晚上看好的房子突然涨价了，最后订了地处南方、同样理想的Bronzeville区的一间（哈哈哈哈看上去更好的）公寓。

关于去哪里

  1. 穷游/知乎/TripAdvisor，以及——Airbnb Guidebook（Guidebook上的地点都是Airbnb的房东们Crowd Sourcing出来的啊，本地人的推荐，值得参考~）。

MingHin氛围很棒，服务态度也非常之好，东西也好吃的，推荐皮蛋瘦肉粥及菠萝流沙包。关键是结账的时候发现四人才消耗八十大刀…内心OS已经十分明显…“三藩真是弱爆了😑”..

Navy Pier/海军码头

饭毕卡路里护体，走在冷风中竟可镇定自若。沿着Michigan Ave走，芝加哥最繁盛的这条街被风格迥异的高楼簇拥，每块石头上的art deco都在讲一个不同的故事。午后阳光好，行道树的树叶绿色还没褪，秋天的颜色却已经涌了上来。我们就在这一片黄黄绿绿的光明里吹着秋风，看一眼千禧公园尽头“腿的盛宴”雕塑，漫步消食，在Navy Pier的世界第一座摩天轮旁晒晒太阳补补钙。

Millennium Park/千禧公园, Jay Pritzker Pavilion/杰.普立兹露天音乐厅, Cloud Gate/云门

补好钙步行去Millennium Park。著名的大豆豆（学名Cloud Gate）就在这里，还有个特别酷的露天音乐厅。

Willis Tower/威利斯大厦

傍晚的时候去了Willis Tower，等了足有两个小时才上到楼顶的Skydeck。Willis Tower在很长一段时间内都是世界第一高楼，占地两个街区，结构叫做”Bundled blocks”，整座大厦由高低不等的九个立方体构成。其实最开始设计的时候设计师抽了几支烟来示意，所以我觉得原生态叫法应该是”Bundeled cigarettes”…

参观摩天大楼的套路大概都大差不差，排队的时候感觉自己穿越回了当时去帝国大厦的情景——排队时候身边的内部陈设和帝国大厦太相似了吧！乘电梯上楼的时候倒是有一点很有趣，电梯上的显示屏会不断提醒你刚才已超越了哪些世界著名建筑的高度。

我们到达楼顶的时候已经七点多了，只能看看夜景。所以强烈推荐早点去排队，傍晚的时候在这儿看看日落估计还是蛮好的，一张票把日景夜景全看了。楼上挺无聊的，一堆人排队在玻璃地板的瞭望台上拍照。我感觉去Willis Tower的全部意义就纯粹是“噢那个地方啊，我去过了”。

Lou Malnati’s/晚餐

第一天晚餐进军芝加哥土特产深盘披萨（Deep dish）。霸霸调研了一圈找到了这家，后来证明果然没选错，口碑不错。我们买了一个half-half的medium，忘记两种口味分别叫什么了，反正有肉的那种巨好吃，没肉的那种巨juicy。

当天有芝加哥本地棒球队的比赛，餐厅里都是个人，于是赶着去看Blues演出的我们直接放弃等位，只好捧着沉甸甸的，real沉甸甸的pizza去旁边的Blues bar。

Buddy Guy’s Legends Blues Bar

之后的事情就更惨了😂…Blues bar里果然不让吃别家的东西，演出九点半开始，我们九点多一点进去已经没有什么位置了。当时四个人已经累得不要不要的，我抱着温暖的大pizza已经连话都不想说了…等了半天，waiter给我们找了四个分开的位置。pizza丢在脚下，累成狗的我呆坐着，和萍萍点了些吃吃喝喝的，已经没有脑容量欣赏著名的芝加哥布鲁斯音乐艺术…

说一下这个Bar，Buddy Guy’s Lengends。去芝加哥之前就听说Chicago Blues非常有名。这Bar是穷游上推荐的，也Google过一些其他不错的Bar，最后还是决定选这个。如果我没那么累的话这个Bar的风格应该是我挺喜欢的那种，不过分吵闹，不乱糟糟，大家坐着，一边吃吃喝喝，一边看看表演，好似回到国内小清吧，轻松惬意。周六票价\$20，平时\$10。我们看的表演是 THE NICK MOSS BAND w/ Omar Coleman，虽然当时累到爆现在已经如断片儿般不记得任何演出内容，但是第一次见识了Blues口琴现场还是挺开心的。

哎，累成狗的时候去接受城市文化熏陶真是有心无力啊。

Oct23

Art Institute of Chicago/芝加哥艺术博物馆

被Trip Advisor评为“世界第一的美术馆”。博物馆正常票价四十多刀，有学生票，便宜一些。

博物馆就在千禧公园旁，门口有两尊非常好辨认的狮像；博物馆对面就是Route 66的起点（照片中间偏左的那个小牌子）——



博物馆馆藏构成和大都会非常相似，画作、雕塑、装饰艺术以及其他类型的一些文物一应俱全。一层是东方文化，二层以欧洲绘画为主，三层为Modern Art & Contemporary Art，负一为摄影展。我先从二层逛起，然后去了三层、负一层，一层逛得比较简略，怕心痛😂，当然主要也是只有四个小时，来不及仔细逛全馆。

欧洲绘画照例按年代排布。中世纪的宗教画一双双死鱼眼；16世纪中叶以后肖像画逐渐增多，题材开始多样化，会出现生活场景和一些景物；17世纪风景画增多，注重场景描绘；18、19世纪开始往印象派走，点彩、印象派开始成为风潮。

说说我全程看到的印象比较深的几幅。首先是梵高这幅自画像。这幅大概是梵高所有自画像中我最喜欢的一幅，然而在展厅里看到的时候更加震撼（说实话应该是超越了当时看到Starry Night原作时的感触，大概审美疲劳）。据说梵高是受点彩的启发画了这幅自画像。背景是很明显的点彩，颜色的搭配让我觉得特别平衡，细看可以看到油彩的凹凸，映着展厅的灯光闪着不同的光泽。

另外就是我超爱的Turner，某展厅正中一幅巨大的Turner，是他最擅长的海洋，暴风中的海洋。另进到一个展厅只扫了一眼就被一幅肖像抓住，那光线明明就写着“伦勃朗”三个大字…着实佩服荷兰叔，都是写实肖像，就他只消看一眼就知道出自他手。啊我改天一定要把《夜巡》给看了…

修拉最著名（我觉得应该是全世界最著名）的点彩《大碗岛的星期天下午》也藏于此。馆藏的莫奈作品不少，考虑到莫奈这么高产画画跟拍照似的，藏品多大概也在情理之中。

我特别喜欢的是侧廊的一系列粉彩（Pastel）肖像，”The Art of Pastel”。本身我最伟大的夙愿之一就是学水彩风景，看到这么柔和的粉彩肖像真是仰慕啊…其中Jean-Baptiste Perronneau的一幅”Portrait of Jean-Baptiste-Antoine Le Moyne”印象最深刻，目前已成为我手机锁屏…



现代艺术有几幅毕加索。当代艺术我没怎么看，赶去负一层看了一下摄影展。摄影馆藏并不丰富，而且大部分是黑白作品，有好些是Great Depression时期的作品。最吸引我的反倒是Thorne Miniature Rooms。这是个微雕馆，里面陈列的都是一些微型室内装饰模型，风格各异。此博物馆比较有趣的一点是，会在一些展区旁放一块牌子，“The Artists of Today”，大概就是说此展厅的艺术品对今天的哪些艺术家影响比较大。比如今天我们熟悉的Wes Anderson，他经常来Throne Miniature Rooms找灵感。于是我在看这些精致的小模型的时候就一直在脑补布达佩斯大饭店…

三楼有一尊非常有趣的雕塑，”Three Men Walking II”。陈设的背景是半透明的帘幕，帘幕彼端是千禧公园和芝加哥的摩天大楼。从展厅看出去就好似这三个人走在芝加哥的天际线上。这布展非常有心。

Al’s Beef/午餐

逛完博物馆之后已经下午三点多了，决定去周边坐坐，等下直接去五点半的游船。我一直想去试一下知乎上有个答主强力推荐的Al’s Beef，据说是芝加哥传统三明治，恰好周围有一家。我点了一个最小号的牛肉三明治，七刀。我一直在质疑Al’s Beef和Al Capone会不会有什么关系，拿了他们家的menu发现这还真是Italian Beef Sandwich…不过至今我还是没搞清楚这家店和芝加哥最有名的黑帮到底是不是有关系…

尝了下味道还不错，特像肉夹馍。仔细研究了下他们家菜单挺有意思的。sandwich根据里肉汁由少到多分为三种，dry, wet, dipped（见下图右栏）。我要了dry，可是肉汁已经很多了…菜单上说dipped是Chicago最local的吃法，基本上就是把整个sandwich放到肉汁里沾满…

Chicago Architecture Foundation River Cruise/建筑游船

我至今参加过的最棒的城市游览项目——Chicago Architecture Foundation River Cruise Aboard Chicago’s First Lady Cruises。据说芝加哥的游船有两种，其一是在密歇根湖看日落，其二是在河道上游览芝加哥的建筑。因为对芝加哥建筑之著名有所耳闻，我们毫不犹豫选了后者。



傍晚七点半的”Twilight Cruises”会稍微贵一点，也许是因为日落时分景致比较好吧。我们大概提前了两三天订，Twilight Cruises的票已经售磬了，我们就买了五点半的。

出发地点是First Lady在三条河流交界处的一个小码头。虽然票上标明了确切的座位，但实际上是大家随便挑座位的，所以建议早点到码头去排队，选个好位置。这艘双层小船楼上楼下都可以坐，我觉得上下层景致各有各的好，大家自己斟酌着选~

上图中的铁桥在芝加哥共有24座，每座都是可以升起的，左右的塔桥就是用于桥的升降。其实每座桥只需要两个塔桥，有些桥有四个塔桥，只是出于美观考虑。铁桥的桥面是不密闭的，要是在桥上不小心掉了手机估计就只能喂河神了…看个细节：

我们的第一艘船上广播坏了，楼下的人听不到讲解员的声音，因此开出去十多分钟就折回去换了艘船。第二次驶出码头的时候几近黄昏，玻璃幕墙上映着暮色。

整个tour一共90分钟，覆盖了三个河道的主要建筑群，讲解员会在全程同步科普各个代表性建筑的故事和历史。观察芝加哥的建筑群，在船上和在街道上真的是完全不同的体验，在船上仰望着林立的高楼慢慢经过，有种人在画中游的感觉。

我们船上的讲解员是个老奶奶，是个建筑史学者，她说她和先生从三藩搬到了芝加哥，就是因为建筑。Tour结束之后她说，”I do not accept tips; I just want to share the stories of Chicago architecture with you.” 我以前去Getty Museum的时候就遇到过退休之后去做讲解员的老奶奶——当时我就觉得，这真是我的理想晚年生活啊…

Café Iberico/晚餐

这一天晚餐大概是我们纠结了最久的…最后决定去这家墨西哥餐厅。事后证明选择没错！！这家店无比靠谱，我们点的每个Tapas都非常好吃。可惜那天没用相机拍，手机又没容量了，没留下什么图片。Tapas点了炸鱿鱼圈，某个虾（虾非常新鲜，咬下去感觉要爆炸），beef stew（这个赞爆了，为本神农氏的点菜水平正了名！牛肉炖得非常酥软但是又不老，不知是何绝技..而且里面的土豆也非常好吃），还有几个忘记是什么了。另外加了一盘Paella（貌似海鲜饭无论在哪里都是要等45分钟），另加了一个提拉米苏（这个甜点厉害了！超级好吃啊，不是特别甜，咖啡浸透的lady finger口感老好了）。

第二天就这样啦。回家训教。

Oct24

The Purple Pig/午餐

米其林推荐的一家店，网上推荐也很多，口碑不错。原计划周日晚上去，一查发现平均等位时间俩小时…我们决定周一中午开门前半小时过去排队。

果然是机智的决定！十一点半开门，我们十一点多到了门口，虽然是周一但门口已经有稀稀落落几个人在排队了。餐厅氛围不错，waiter不穿制服，以至于我们一开始还以为来帮我们点菜的是这家店的owner…

这顿饭真是吃得心满意足，每道菜都很不错，没有踩雷。

Wicker Park/柳条公园

吃晚饭我们去了号称“芝加哥南锣鼓巷”的Wicker Park。据说此地小众设计师店/古着店/涂鸦小街/总之各种文青喜欢的东西云集。之前还看过一种说法是，北部才是芝加哥真正的精神所在。可惜我们时间不多，没有再往北走。

之前被安利过The Wormhole Coffee，这里就成了我们的第一站。哎午饭吃太饱了，没体验他们家咖啡。周围的人，带吉他的，穿着鼓囊囊旧夹克的，顶着一蓬长发的，聊着天的，晒着太阳的。如今回想起这里，脑海里突然蹦出来我雷的《鼓楼》——执着的，迷惘的，文艺青年很多。

N Milwaukee Ave上古着店特多——真的好多！要是我住芝加哥估计一到周末就会跑过来啊啊啊。据说Kizuki Ramen & Izakaya的拉面不错，我的Airbnb Host还给我们推荐了Big Star和The Violet Hour。另外值得一提的是Myopic Books——晶晶看到路边这家书店，果断把我们仨都拽了进去——结果发现，这地方特别酷！书架填满了两层楼的墙壁，古老粗糙，特别有Harry Potter的画风。楼下还有个小地下室，也装满了书。在回家的路上我们发现这家店竟然是芝加哥最大的二手书店~



附一个王师傅文艺的后脑勺——

N Damen Ave据说也很值得一逛，但我们去的时候好多店都没开门，街上有些冷清。

Chicagos Home of Chicken & Waffles/晚餐

晚餐我们就近解决，这是Airbnb Host推荐的一家店。鉴于我神农氏的美名，我提出去这家店的时候并没有跟三位宝宝讲明这家是Soul Food（就是美国南部黑人常吃的菜品），笑。不过这家店还是挺不错的，主营炸鸡和华夫饼。他们家的yam很好吃，就是太甜了一点。

买好打包回家。啊于是又到了我最喜爱的人生时刻！有酒有肉有电影有沙发！！！！！😍！吃饱喝足抱着Netflix看了一晚上，旅程在终章归于平静。

Oct25

归程

到了回家的日子。11.35am的飞机，一早就收到房东的信息，我说一切都挺好（此处省略大段诚挚的感谢）希望有机会再见咯。

谢谢晶晶，萍萍，霸霸，让我拍脑袋的决定变成现实。真希望！我们四个能建立长期旅行伙伴关系！一起走最远的路，喝最烈的气泡水😉。

总结陈词

来美国两年多，似乎一直没有再做个serious traveler。行前疏于学习，行毕疏于记录。开Blog的初衷之一就是认真记录人生，所以早就决定从这次去芝加哥开始，以后的每次旅行无论大小，用心做攻略，认真去了解、用心去铭记每个陌生的地方。学着关心旅伴，做个能担事情也能为他人着想的人。想起来第一次独自出门的时候是十七岁夏天，那时还是个热衷于写装逼游记的小孩儿，有“很执着的旅行信条”。这么多年过去了，万事都在变，可还是应该像最开始那样，怀着认真的态度去路过世界的每个角落啊。

回程的那个晚上过的真是辛苦。黎明已到浙江境内，因为三个小台风聚首，大家都在猜测今天会不会下雨。

可是车窗外是难得的清景。土地平坦、延展，稻田青翠. 巨大的云层翻卷，边缘点染淡淡的金色。天空还没有完全亮起，看得见粉紫色暮霭。

在火车上欣赏，真像是在看色彩清淡的文艺片…

车里挤满去上学的学生，聊些以后的事情。

我刚离开的地方不是他城，不是陌生而冰冷的地方。想到它，我会想起我坐过的公交和地铁，我逛过的街市与胡同，想到我曾在这个俏皮而沉稳的城市生活过的一周，想到有类似于亲人的人，他们在送别时对我说，我们明年这儿见。

  • box-sizing property
    Whether width and height properties (and min/max properties) includes only the content(content-box) or also include border & padding(border-box). Applied to elements with width & height.
  • initial keyword
    Set a CSS property to its default value. Can be used for any CSS property, and on any HTML element.
  • inherit keyword
    Specifies that a property should inherit its value from its parent element. Can be used for any CSS property, and on any HTML element.
  • Set up element’s background
    • background-image: url(...)
      Apply to body element to set the background image of the page.
    • background-size: contain
      auto & initial(image’s real size); <length> & <percentage>(px or percent of the parent element; for percentage, if only one value is given, the second one would be set as auto); cover(image as large as possible even there’s an overflow); contain(image as large as possible but stretch within the content area). EXAMPLE
    • background-position: 50% 50%;
      Sets the starting position(in percentage or in px) of a background image. Default: bg-image is placed at the top-left corner of an element, and repeated both vertically and horizontally.
    • background-repeat: no-repeat;
      Repeat or not in x/y axis.

    • relative: the element will be positioned relative to its default position(AKA its static position).

Delivering Web Fonts

How to setup the fonts in browser

  • @font-face
    • Set the font in css; have to specify the font file’s URL; need to consider browser compatibility and font optimization(which font to load first).
    • Example and explanation
  • Web Font Services
    Take care of font optimization, browser compatibility, reliable performance, and licensing agreements behind the scenes.
    1. Google Fonts
      By adding one line of <link> element in HTML.
    2. Typekit
      By adding JavaScripts to specify typekit font’s js file.
  • Web Font Loader
    • Web Font Services are convinient for web font delivery, but not the ideal methods. Problem: the fonts are referenced in the head of the page. Chrome, Firefox, Opera, and Safari, text won’t render the page until custom fonts are loaded, resulting into flash of invisible text (FOIT). Internet Explorer displays the fallback type first and then switches to custom type once the font assets finish loading, resulting in a glaring flash of unstyled text (FOUT).
    • Web Font Loader provides an optimal experience across modern browsers. It loads fonts asynchronously and cache fonts in browser. Users may see FOUT the first time they visit the site, but their experience won’t be interrupted once the font assets are downloaded and cached.
    • Example and explanation
  • Font Face Observer
    • Web Font Loader solves the FOIT issue, but doesn’t handle the FOUT issue.
    • Font Face Observer can be referenced at the end of document to avoid the extra overhead. Uses JavaScript’s scroll events to load and monitor web fonts.

Selecting Body Text

Basic font features need to be considered when choosing font for body text

  • Generous X-height
    X-height is an indicator of the sizes’ harmony between lower and upper case letters. It is the vertical measure of a lowercase x, which is usually bigger than half of the cap height(the distance between the baseline and the top of the capital letter.). The shorter the x-height, the smaller the letter will appear, which can cause the type to become illegible. It can neither be too large, as n and h will be difficult to distinguish.
  • Open Apertures
    Apertures are the opening gaps found in certain letters such as c, e, and s. Closed apetures are bad for legibility. c can be mistaken for o at smaller sizes.
  • Even Spacing
    Letters should not be too close to or too far away from each other; good for readability.
  • Clear Terminals
    Terminals are the end of the letters a, c, f, j, r, y. Distinguishable terminals make the letters more readable.
  • Distinguishable Ascenders and Descenders
    Ascenders refers to the strokes that extend above the mean line and descenders refer to the strokes that extend below the baseline. The more distinguishable the ascenders and descenders are, the more legible the letters.
  • Styles & Weights
    For a family of type to work as body text, there need to be at least 4 basic styles: regular, italic, bold, and bold italic.
  • Contrast
    Contrast refers to how different between the thick and thin strokes of a letter. High contract letters are good for posters and advertising, but might not be legible enough for long-term reading.

Choosing Headings

How to choose suitable typefaces or customize the sizes for headings

  • It’s recommended to use the same typeface for headings and body text for visual consistency and better browser loading performance.
  • If different typefaces must be mixed, it’s not recommended to use two typefaces that are fundamentally similar but still different (e.g. two serifs or two sans-serifs). It’s recommended to conbine typefaces in a same superfamily—typefaces that have different classes (e.g. sans, serif, slab) but share similar structure. Another suggestion is using typefaces from the same designer.
  • Recommend to use em but not px.

吸气压音原理：正常情况下，十孔口气吸气时吸气簧(draw reed plate)震动，呼气时呼气簧(blow reed plate)震动。吸气压音则是通过吸气使上方的呼气簧震动，从而吹出比正常吸气音低半音的音符。

卖身契

我有工作了。
本是想发条朋友圈马一下，可觉得有太多话可说，又不便说，最后便不知说什么。

对于Airbnb，我心里充满感激。三百份简历里挑一个intern，而我是最后一百人中50%的engineering intern，也是其中20%的master/phd intern，除了幸运就只剩语塞了。Intern的八十一天里，遇到了合适的project，做事尽了心力，敢说自己没有愧对这份幸运。拿到return offer的第二天清晨，我醒来听着SoMA的鸟叫，感觉很多年以来的焦虑一点一点从身上消失，散去——患得患失的日子就此结束吧。这是我实习的第八十一天。

2012年春天我大一快毕业，选专业。自此之后的很多年，世界变了很多，可质疑、后悔、困惑始终对我不离不弃。我的专业，我不喜欢，我不适合，可我逃不开。如果你的天赋，你生活的热忱，和使你饱足的职业，它们是同一件事该有多好。

有人说二十岁到二十五岁是人生最迷惘的日子，我想是的。

还好二十五岁到来之前，我就已从这深潭中浮了出来。从选了专业开始，我就一直避免去想我终有一天要去工作的事实——IT圈会有适合我且我喜欢做的事情吗。直到我开始意识到我可以去Airbnb——这里有使我饱足的职业；这个人人都有隐藏技能的地方，给员工发现代艺术博物馆年票的地方，文艺到被打趣成设计公司的地方，也许我不必牺牲我臆想中自己拥有的天赋；而且，在这个旅行公司，有什么理由不去热爱生活呢。

本来以为自己永远遇不到对的人，突然发现其实他也喜欢你，大概就是这一年冬去春来时我经历的一切吧。
所以我觉得Airbnb于我而言有一种超出工作范畴的意义，它不仅让我找到了面包，还予我以收集星星的机会。它的culture让我敢去找自己，让我觉得自己那些与本业毫无瓜葛的夙愿都值得去实现。也正因为如此，签完offer那一刻的我感到无比惶恐——怕自己不够好，怕自己配不上我喜欢和珍惜的它。

每次说“我在Airbnb”都会很自豪。
每次听别人说“我也觉得Airbnb特适合你”都会偷偷开心很久。
而剩下的124天，我要学着做个很Airbnb的人。
加油。
